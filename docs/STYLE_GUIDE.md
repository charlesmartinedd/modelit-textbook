# Style Guide

## Connected: How Systems Shape Our World

This style guide ensures consistency across all chapters of the textbook.

---

## üìê Typography

### Font Families

- **Body Text:** Georgia or Palatino (serif), 14pt
- **Headers:** Sans-serif (Arial/Helvetica), various sizes
- **Code/Technical:** Monospace (Courier New), 12pt

### Header Hierarchy

- **H1 (Chapter Title):** 42pt, bold, primary blue (#0F6ACE)
- **H2 (Section Title):** 28pt, bold, dark gray (#333333)
- **H3 (Subsection):** 20pt, semi-bold, dark gray (#333333)
- **H4 (Minor Heading):** 16pt, bold, dark gray (#333333)

### Text Formatting

- **Emphasis:** *Italic* for emphasis
- **Strong:** **Bold** for key terms and important concepts
- **Key Terms:** Bold on first use, followed by definition
- **Quotes:** 12pt, italic, indented with left border

## üé® Color Palette

### Primary Colors

- **Primary Blue:** `#0F6ACE` - Main brand color, headers, accents
- **Cyan Accent:** `#48d2fc` - Highlights, callouts, secondary elements

### Text Colors

- **Primary Text:** `#000000` (black) or `#333333` (dark gray)
- **Secondary Text:** `#666666` (medium gray)
- **Muted Text:** `#999999` (light gray)

### Background Colors

- **Page Background:** `#FFFFFF` (white)
- **Callout Boxes:** `#F0F8FF` (light blue tint)
- **Activity Boxes:** `#FFF9E6` (light yellow tint)

### Usage Guidelines

- Use primary blue for major headings and important callouts
- Use cyan for highlights and interactive elements
- Maintain sufficient contrast for readability (WCAG AA minimum)

## üìê Layout & Spacing

### Page Margins

- **Top/Bottom:** 1 inch (72pt)
- **Left/Right:** 0.75 inch (54pt)
- **Gutter:** 0.25 inch (18pt) for binding

### Spacing

- **Paragraph Spacing:** 12pt after paragraphs
- **Section Spacing:** 24pt before H2, 18pt before H3
- **List Spacing:** 6pt between items
- **Image Spacing:** 12pt above and below

### Column Layout

- **Single Column:** Standard text width
- **Two Column:** For sidebars and callouts
- **Full Width:** For images and diagrams

---

## üñºÔ∏è Images & Graphics

### Image Specifications

- **Resolution:** Minimum 300 DPI for print quality
- **Format:** PNG or JPG (high quality)
- **Embedding:** Base64-encoded in HTML for PDF reliability
- **Alt Text:** Required for all images (accessibility)

### Image Types

1. **Educational Illustrations:** Clear, age-appropriate, engaging
2. **Diagrams:** Simple, labeled, easy to understand
3. **Charts/Graphs:** Clear labels, appropriate colors
4. **Photographs:** High quality, relevant to content

### Image Placement

- **Full Width:** For major concepts and chapter openers
- **Half Width:** For supporting illustrations
- **Sidebar:** For callouts and additional context
- **Inline:** Small icons and decorative elements

### Caption Style

- **Font:** 11pt, italic, gray (#666666)
- **Format:** "Figure X.X: [Description]"
- **Placement:** Below image, centered

## üìù Writing Style

### Tone & Voice

- **Tone:** Engaging, clear, encouraging
- **Voice:** Active voice preferred
- **Person:** Second person ("you") or third person ("students")
- **Formality:** Conversational but educational

### Language Guidelines

- **Age-Appropriate:** Grades 5-8 reading level
- **Clarity:** Short sentences, clear explanations
- **Vocabulary:** Define new terms on first use
- **Examples:** Concrete, relatable, real-world

### Paragraph Structure

- **Length:** 3-5 sentences per paragraph
- **Topic Sentence:** Clear main idea
- **Supporting Sentences:** Details and examples
- **Transition:** Smooth flow between paragraphs

## üìã Content Structure

### Chapter Elements

1. **Chapter Opener**
   - Engaging title
   - Chapter overview
   - Learning objectives
   - Key questions

2. **Main Content**
   - Introduction
   - Core concepts (with examples)
   - Activities and exercises
   - Real-world connections

3. **Chapter Closer**
   - Summary
   - Key terms review
   - Review questions
   - Further exploration

### Section Organization

- **Introduction:** Hook and context
- **Core Content:** Main concepts
- **Examples:** Concrete illustrations
- **Activities:** Hands-on learning
- **Connections:** Real-world applications

## üéØ Special Elements

### Callout Boxes

- **"Think About It":** Light blue background (#F0F8FF)
- **"Try This":** Light yellow background (#FFF9E6)
- **"Key Concept":** Border with primary blue accent
- **"Real World":** Light green background (#F0FFF0)

### Lists

- **Bulleted Lists:** For items without order
- **Numbered Lists:** For steps or sequences
- **Definition Lists:** For key terms

### Tables

- **Headers:** Bold, primary blue background
- **Rows:** Alternating light gray for readability
- **Borders:** Thin, gray lines
- **Spacing:** Adequate padding

## üìö References & Citations

### Citation Format

- **In-Text:** (Author, Year) or (Source Name)
- **Bibliography:** APA style for educational materials
- **Web Sources:** Include URL and access date

### Reference Style

- **Books:** Author (Year). *Title*. Publisher.
- **Websites:** Organization (Year). *Page Title*. URL
- **Images:** Photographer/Artist (Year). *Image Title*. Source.
